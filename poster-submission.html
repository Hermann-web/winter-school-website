Poster Submissions

Showcase Your Research at the Spring School: Artificial Intelligence & Health!

The Spring School: Artificial Intelligence & Health invites participants to submit posters.

Posters shall present innovative research, practical applications, or pioneering methodologies in artificial intelligence, data science, and their intersection with healthcare and beyond.

// Topics icon

Topics of Interest

We encourage submissions that explore:

  • AI for Health: diagnosis, treatment planning, digital health, etc.
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning methods
  • Data Science in Biomedical, Social, or Industrial contexts
  • Ethics, Bias, and Interpretability in AI
  • Multimodal and Large Language Models
  • Real-world AI Applications across sectors
// Calendar icon

Submission Deadline

All submissions must be received by February 15, 2025.

// Award icon

Selection Process

A jury of experts will evaluate all posters based on innovation, scientific rigor and relevance to AI and its applications especially healhcare. xx posters will be selected for the event.

// Gift icon

Waived Registration Fees

Selected posters will benefit from waived Winter School registration fees, providing you complimentary access to the event and its exclusive sessions.

// Presentation icon

Poster Session

Selected posters will be printed at no cost and displayed during the poster session, providing opportunities for in-depth discussions and networking with distinguished attendees, including AI pioneers like Yann LeCun and Michael Jordan.
